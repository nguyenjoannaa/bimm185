About

Drug abuse and addictions are rising amongst the younger population. Although there are websites out there dedicated to the prevention of such, those websites seem to be more information heavy. What's Popping looks to provide an interactive learning environment to stop and prevent future addiction problems.

Statistics

In the United States, the abuse of tobacco, alcohol, and illicit drugs cost roughly around $600 billion in relation to crimes, work efficiency and healthcare. 52 million people in the United States over the age of 12 have used prescription drugs for non-medical reasons in their lifetime. With numbers high as this, it is important to spread awareness on an issue that is affecting society.
